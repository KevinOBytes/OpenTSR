# Vocabulary

OpenTSR vocabulary terms are documented in [`spec/vocabulary.md`](https://github.com/KevinOBytes/OpenTSR/blob/main/spec/vocabulary.md).

## Current Namespaced Terms

- `physical:sensor`: structured physical telemetry attached under `payload`.

## Why It Matters

This vocabulary enables direct correlation between cyber and physical signals in the same event envelope.
