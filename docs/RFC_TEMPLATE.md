# RFC: Short Descriptive Name

## Motivation

Why is this change needed? Describe the pain, risk, or missed capability.

## Scope

What is in scope and what is explicitly out of scope?

## Proposal

Describe the proposed schema/semantic/profile changes in concrete terms.

## Compatibility

Is this change backward compatible? If not, explain what breaks.

## Security Impact

Describe effects on integrity, confidentiality, availability, and abuse resistance.

## Examples

Provide representative payloads before and after the proposed change.

## Migration Notes

Describe migration strategy for producers and consumers.

## Open Questions

List unresolved design questions that need maintainer guidance.
